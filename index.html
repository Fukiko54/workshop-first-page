Out of the World

Professional photography that features inanimate object matter is known as still-life photography. Conveying the specific messages with still-life objects to the audience may be difficult, but it is the way to explore mine out of the world.


The first pictures show the mom and her kids having a fun conversation. I was particular about the position of the cranes, such as making the children look like they were talking with their parents facing up, and putting the child on the wing of the parent to express their childhood.
